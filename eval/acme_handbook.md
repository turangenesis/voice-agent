# Acme Corp Employee Handbook

## Time off
- Full-time employees receive **20 days** of paid vacation per year.
- Employees get **10 paid sick days** per year.
- Annual performance reviews happen every **December**.

## Work arrangements
- Employees may work remotely up to **3 days per week** with manager approval.
- The probationary period for new hires is **90 days**.

## Facilities & benefits
- The office parking garage code is **4471**.
- The company 401(k) match is **4 percent** of salary.
- Expense reimbursements are processed within **30 days** of submission.

## Contacts & offboarding
- HR contact email is **hr@acme.co**.
- Laptops must be returned within **5 business days** of departure.
